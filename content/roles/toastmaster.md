# Toastmaster

The role of the Toastmaster is to run the meeting.

## Flow

1. Joke
1. General Business
1. Speeches
1. Table topics
1. Evaluations

## Theme

A theme helps add interest to the meeting.

Success themes in the past include:

1. Rugby world cup
1. Something else
1. Bla bla bla
